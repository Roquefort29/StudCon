ABOUT US

ABOUT US

Our organization's mission is to help and support students, as the knowledge acquiring process may become stressful and challenging.

StudCon can help students overcome academic and non-academic problems, avoiding poor consequences.

Additionally, we host a wide range of events every week.

Team games, entertaining activities, consultations with professional psychologists and educational advisors.

StudCon is non-profit organization supported by donations and governmental funding.

We are happy to help to every learner of our university to overcome problems and achieve desired goals.
